### What AGISTI Does

AGISTI enables LLMs to improve themselves without any external teacher, reward model, or human feedback. The model:

1. Probes its own competency across domains
2. Generates problems targeting its weaknesses
3. Analyzes activation differences between correct and incorrect solutions
4. Proposes LoRA-style weight modifications via SVD decomposition
5. Validates every change against an independent benchmark (QuickBench)
6. Accepts improvements and rolls back regressions — automatically

This is **not distillation** (no teacher model) and **not RLHF** (no human evaluator). The model is both the student and the surgeon.
